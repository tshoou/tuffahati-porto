APP SCREENS COMING SOON

Introducing solids is a major milestone in a baby’s development. Around 6 months of age, babies begin transitioning from an exclusively milk-based diet to a combination of breast milk or formula and complementary foods.

At this stage, nutrition becomes even more important — particularly nutrients like iron, zinc, and key vitamins and minerals. At the same time, feeding is not just about nutrition. It is also about developing coordination, exploring textures, and learning how to eat.

Key Focus: Simple & Nutrient-Dense

This app focuses on simple, nutrient-dense meals that support both nutrition and development, without overcomplicating the process.

Breast milk or formula should still remain the primary source of nutrition throughout the first year, with solid foods gradually increasing over time as babies build confidence, skill, and appetite. Working closely with a paediatric nutritionist, I designed this app to guide parents intuitively through this crucial developmental stage.
